Meetings in Hameenlinna
Versatile conference facilities in the city centre

Hame Hotel's unique conference facilities provide a modern setting for meetings from 5 up to 300 people

Why arrange a meeting at Hame Hotel? Hame Hotel offers pleasant, efficient and versatile meeting, course, seminar or training rooms in the heart of Hameenlinna, 200 metres from the Central Railway Station. Altogether, the conference rooms have space for over 450 people. Professional and friendly staff will help with all the meeting arrangements. With an atmosphere of over 100 years, the Art Nouveau-style conference annex is unique. Crystal chandeliers, antique furniture, stained glass windows, bay windows, portraits and paintings on the walls, upright pianos, and grand pianos create a historical atmosphere like no other. Versatile meeting breakfast from the coffee buffet, a meeting lunch at the restaurant’s buffet, and afternoon coffee with sweet pastries and fruit are included in the full day meeting package.
